Android App Portfolio

EmojiCam

Turns photos into Emoji mosaics

  • Visual effects app that turns photos into Emoji mosaics
  • Works with Android camera APIs to take photos and process them
  • Users can also load photos from their device

Occultation Tracker

Allows astronomers to calculate the local peak time of occultations

  • Occultations occur when an asteroid passes in front of a star
  • Users can tap on the map to calculate the local peak time of these events
  • Alternatively, they can select their current location

eVscope Calibrator

Allows eVscope users to set the time on their telescope

  • Algorithm sets telescope time with a precision of +/- 10 ms
  • Uses device GPS to obtain high-accurary time data
  • Also sets the location and altitude on telescope
  • Allows observation data to be used to contribute to "citizen science" campaigns

Observation Log

Makes logging scientific observations easy

  • Auto-generated observation timestamps
  • Observation headers including Date, Location, Attendees and Conditions
  • Easily export observations to .csv files
  • Especially useful for astronomy

BaseWatch

Customizable notifications for your favorite MLB team

  • Algorithm detects exciting game moments
  • Perfect for busy fans who still want to keep track of their team
  • Notification preferences are customizable by adjusting the "Buzz Threshold"

Rotary

Intuitive, fast-paced game

  • Place circles on the spinning wheel to complete each level
  • 32 challenging levels
  • Endless Mode challenges users to beat each other's high scores
